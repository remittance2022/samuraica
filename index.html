How to mint

1. I already have a wallet

  • Click on “Connect Wallet”

  • Choose how many Samurai Cats NFT you want (maximum 20 per wallet)

  • Click on MINT

  • Another screen will open in your wallet to accept and sign the transaction.

  • Once it’s done you are minting your Samurai Cats!

Refresh the screen if you don’t see them coming

2. I don't have a wallet

  • Start opening a wallet. Few are compatible with our site but we recommend Metamask wallet.
    You can download it directly from this link
    https://metamask.io/download

  • Add Ethereum to your wallet

  • Almost done, continue to the instruction number 1
